Privacy Policy for UML Diagram Generator Plugin

Last Updated: 2023-06-01

This privacy policy describes how the UML Diagram Generator Plugin ("we", "our", or "the plugin") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

The UML Diagram Generator Plugin collects the following information:
- Diagram code/text that you provide for generation
- Basic usage statistics (number of requests, types of diagrams generated)
- Technical information necessary to provide the service

We do not collect or store:
- Personal identifying information
- User credentials or authentication tokens
- Chat history before or after the diagram generation request

2. How We Use Information

We use the information collected to:
- Generate diagrams based on your specifications
- Improve the plugin's functionality and performance
- Debug issues that may arise during usage
- Monitor usage patterns to optimize service delivery

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or lease your information to third parties. Your diagram code is processed through our service and may be processed by Kroki.io or other diagram generation backends as needed to fulfill your request.

4. Data Retention

Diagram code and generated images are temporarily stored only for the duration needed to process your request and are automatically deleted afterward. We may retain anonymized usage statistics for service improvement purposes.

5. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the information we collect and maintain. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@example.com.
